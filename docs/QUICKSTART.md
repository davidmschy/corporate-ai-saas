Get your AI Workforce running in 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- Whop account with active Corporate AI subscription
- Modern web browser

## Step 1: Access Your Dashboard

After purchase, you'll receive a welcome email with your dashboard link:

```
https://dashboard.corporateai.work/setup?customer=YOUR_ID
```

Or access via: https://dashboard.corporateai.work

## Step 2: Choose Your Infrastructure

Select how you want to deploy your AI workforce:

| Option | Best For | Setup Time |
|--------|----------|------------|
| **Cloudflare Workers** | Most companies | Instant |
| **Mac Mini** | High security requirements | 1-2 days shipping |
| **H100 Clusters** | Maximum performance | Enterprise setup |

**Default:** Cloudflare Workers (recommended for most users)

## Step 6: Advanced Usage

### Multi-Agent Workflows
Delegate complex projects across multiple agents:

**Example: Product Launch**
1. CEO creates launch strategy
2. CMO develops marketing plan
3. CSO prepares sales enablement
4. CTO ensures technical readiness
5. Board reviews for risks

### Board Meetings
Schedule monthly "Board meetings" with your AI directors:
- Review financial performance (Finance Director)
- Assess cybersecurity posture (Cybersecurity Director)
- Evaluate strategic initiatives (Lead Independent Director)

### Custom Training (Scale/Enterprise)
Teach agents your company specifics:
- Upload brand guidelines
- Share previous board decks
- Provide product documentation
- Import financial historicals

## Best Practices

### 1. Start Small
Begin with 1-2 agents, add more as you get comfortable.

### 2. Be Specific
Good: "Analyze our Q3 marketing spend and suggest 3 cost optimizations"
Bad: "Help with marketing"

### 3. Provide Context
Share relevant documents, previous work, and constraints.

### 4. Review & Iterate
AI agents improve with feedback. Correct mistakes and explain why.

### 5. Maintain Human Oversight
AI agents recommend; humans decide. Never delegate critical decisions fully.

## Troubleshooting

### Agent Not Responding
- Check your API request limit
- Verify integration connections
- Check status page: status.corporateai.work

### Incorrect Responses
- Provide more context
- Correct the agent and explain
- Use custom training (Scale+)

### Integration Issues
- Re-authorize the connection
- Check API key permissions
- Contact support
